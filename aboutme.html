About Me

I grew up in sunny Orlando, Florida, where I graduated from the University of Central Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology and a minor in Anthropological Archaeology. I moved to Eugene, Oregon in 2019 to continue my archaeological studies at the University of Oregon.

When I'm not digging in middens in the Caribbean or cataloguing bones in the lab, you can find me at the gym, deadlifting 500+ lbs while ranting about Ponce De Leon.

My collections of shiny objects put corvids to shame; ask me about my fountain pens, sneakers, or the antique WWII-era watch that I restored after my grandfather found it in a trash pile.
